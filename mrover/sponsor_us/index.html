Sponsorships

MRover relies on sponsorships and donations to fund all of our activities throughout the year. In particular, we use funds to:
  • purchase materials
  • create prototypes
  • improve our toolset
  • organize outreach events
  • send the team to competition
As we ramp up our outreach efforts, plan more prototypes, and look into going to additional competitions, our team budget is rising. We would appreciate your support to help us continue our growth as a team.

For more information about sponsoring us, please contact us at:

rover@umich.edu

Individual Donations

We value online donations by individuals. You choose the size of your donation. All donors get our newsletter and their name on our website.
