Sponsorships

MRover relies on sponsorships and donations to fund all of our activities throughout the year. In particular, we use funds to:
  • purchase materials
  • create prototypes
  • improve our toolset
  • organize outreach events
  • send the team to competition
As we ramp up our outreach efforts, plan more prototypes, and look into going to additional competitions, our team budget is rising. We would appreciate your support to help us continue our growth as a team.

For more information about sponsoring us, please contact us at:

rover@umich.edu

Donations

We value online donations by individuals. You choose the size of your donation. All donors get our newsletter and their name on our website.

Sponsorship Levels

Platinum - $5,000 or greater

  • Includes all Gold, Silver, and Bronze benefits plus the following:
  • Access to the team for videos, case studies, etc.
  • Featured logo on our rover during competition.
  • Most prominent logos on website, t-shirts, and presentations

Gold - $2,500 or greater

  • Includes all Silver and Bronze benefits plus the following:
  • Exclusive access to recruiting events hosted by MRover.
  • Primary logos on website, t-shirts, and presentations
  • Social media post recognizing/thanking the sponsor.

Silver - $1000

  • Includes all Bronze benefits plus the following:
  • Secondary logo on website, t-shirts, and presentations
  • Exclusive meetings with the MRover team

Bronze - $500

  • Resume book containing resumes of all team members
  • Tertiary logo on website, t-shirts, and all official presentations
  • Monthly updates via email newsletter

We are also willing to accomodate non-monetary donations.
